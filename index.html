Crystals Collector

Instructions

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

Each of the four crystals below carries a specific number of points. Their values are hidden until you click on the crystals.

By clicking on a crystal, you will receive the corresponding points, which will be added to your total score.

If your total score matches the randomly generated number, you win the game. If your total score exceeds the randomly generated number, you lose the game.

After each win or loss, the game starts over.
